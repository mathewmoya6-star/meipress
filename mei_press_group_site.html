Who we are
About Mei Press Group

Mei Press Group is a Nairobi-based consultancy dedicated to empowering individuals and organisations across East Africa and beyond. Founded on the belief that every person deserves access to world-class opportunities, we have spent over eight years connecting people with study abroad programmes, safety training, and professional placement services.

Our team of experienced consultants walks with every client from first inquiry through to successful placement — providing guidance, paperwork support, and ongoing follow-up every step of the way.
